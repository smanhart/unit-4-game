Crystal Collecter Game

You will be given a ramdom number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until it is clicked.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal changes.
